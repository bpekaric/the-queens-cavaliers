## Climbing Gears

:::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::

Climbing gears are worn as a harness and operated with two hands, allowing you to move between areas which are normally not passable.
